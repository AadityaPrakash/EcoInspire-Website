ENVIRONMENTAL QUIZ

As the name suggests, this quiz will test the knowledge of our participants about our environment.

Register

About Event

Introduction:

A quiz is a form of a game or mind sport, in which the players (as individuals or in teams) attempt to answer questions correctly. It is a game to test your knowledge about a certain subject. A quiz is a brief assessment used in education and similar fields to measure growth in knowledge, abilities and skills. They are usually scored in points.
The quiz allows participants to:
  • Enhance knowledge regarding the topic
  • Be up-to-date about current issues
  • Think and answer quickly
  • Improve mind, speech and knowledge coordination

Theme:

This quiz is based on the topics related to environment which may include topics related to green energy, rainwater harvesting, energy conservation, renewable energy, recycling, pollution, soil quality, greenhouse effect, climate change, water purification, waste management, etc. and most importantly the steps to be taken at individual level.

Event Guidelines:

  • Only TEAM entries of 2 people
  • Answer in your own turn.
  • The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
  • No negative points
  • Four rounds in total
  • Equal points for each question
  • If any team doesnâ€™t answer, the question will be passed to any team answers
  • Only 1 chance for a right answer
  • In case of equal points, a tiebreaker round will be conducted
  • No use of electronic devices
  • The format of the questions can be objective, multiple choice, true/false
  • Replacement of a team member is not allowed after registration

Registration details

  • Participate in teams of 2 people each
  • Total 20 teams
  • Class 7-9th
  • Total registrations: 40

Judgment Criteria

  • Fairly based on the scores.
  • In case of a tie (2 or more participants have the same score), a tie-breaker question will be asked.

Why Join

To Promote the importance of Green Energy for a sustainable environment.

To bring environmental education (EE) out of the classrooms

To engage students and make it fun for them

To encourage students to think about current environmental problems and possible solutions

To provide a relaxed environment for children to interact and learn from their peers
