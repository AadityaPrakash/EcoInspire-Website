Competitions Details

Battle of WORDS (DEBATE)

The debate competition namely “Battle of Words” will be an interesting exchange of arguments by our participants regarding current environmental issues. Students will get the opportunity to critically analyze the issue and present their arguments, honing their debating skills.

Read More

COLORAMA (Poster Making)

The poster making competition called “Colorama”, will showcase the creative skills of our participants in the process of learning about ways to care for our mother earth. Students will have to engage with their creative sides to put their ideas and convey them through posters.

Read More

VISION 2050 (Symposium)

The symposium is called “Vision 2050”, where students will have to discuss a case study presented to them prior to the day of the event to come up with innovate solutions to address the case studies and finally present them during the event.

Read More

Greenovate

“Greenovate” phrased by members of our team, aims to create awareness amongst students about the amount of waste generated in this present day and age, while teaching them how this waste can be recycled by creating beautiful crafts.

Read More

NUKKAD NATAK (STREET PLAY)

The street play translated in Hindi as “Nukkad Natak”, is an attempt to give students a platform to enact existing or foreseen environmental problems in a way they see fit to make other people aware about it.

Read More

ENVIRONMENTAL QUIZ

As the name suggests, this quiz will test the knowledge of our participants about our environment.

Read More

Rangoli competition

Rangoli Competitions play an important role in motivating students to perform and outshine in their creative skills. It offers a chance for participants to gain substantial experience, adopt innovative techniques, showcase talents, analyze and evaluate outcomes and uncover personal ability.

Read More

TEACH FOR GREEN EXHIBITION

This exhibition will showcase the working models using solar energy and recycled craft items built our students, who have undergone the Green School Green Community Program during the last academic year.

Read More

PANEL DISCUSSION

The panel discussion is intended more to address the bigger issues regarding environmental education, it’s implementation in schools and the way forward to improve it. Our participants will be persons who have been in the environmental field for a long period of time, who have the expertise to comment on the topic.

Why Join

To Promote the importance of Green Energy for a sustainable environment.

To bring environmental education (EE) out of the classrooms

To engage students and make it fun for them

To encourage students to think about current environmental problems and possible solutions

To provide a relaxed environment for children to interact and learn from their peers
