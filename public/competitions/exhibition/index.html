EXHIBITION

This exhibition will showcase the working models using solar energy and recycled craft items built our students, who have undergone the Green School Green Community Program during the last academic year.

About

Introduction

Teach For Green Exhibition is a platform for students from schools where we run our Green School Green Community program can showcase the models they made during the program. Teach for Green has a vision of to promote the importance of green energy and sustainable practices for a better environment through various activities in the field of training and skill development throughout India.

Competition Guidelines:

  • Students from Teach For Green schools will showcase their models
  • Students will highlight their journey and explain their models
  • Guests and participants will be allowed to stroll through the stalls at allotted time
  • The 3 best and most innovative models will be given prizes

Why Join

To Promote the importance of Green Energy for a sustainable environment.

To bring environmental education (EE) out of the classrooms

To engage students and make it fun for them

To encourage students to think about current environmental problems and possible solutions

To provide a relaxed environment for children to interact and learn from their peers
